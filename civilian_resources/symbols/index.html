About the flag

The flag of Bergsland is a dark blue (#658FED) field, charged with a white (#FFFFFF) fimbriated light blue (#7DC8FF) Nordic Cross, extending to the edges, with a 5-pointed star in the top right quadrant. The vertical part of the cross is shifted to the hoist side, and the horizontal part of the cross is shifted to the bottom.
